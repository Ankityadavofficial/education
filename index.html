How it works

Our Learning Library is designed with flexibility, so it can be tailored to your child's unique interests and needs.
They can follow it step-by-step, jump around out of order, or dive deep into favorite areas.

A safe space for trying.

Our Guided Lessons include carefully sequenced activities filled with characters kids love. They can hone 800+ core skills, like subtraction and short vowels, through fun songs, stories, worksheets, and games. So much fun, they won’t even realize they’re practicing key skills for school!

Assess and address.

As your kid plays their way through Guided Lessons, their scores are tracked in our handy Progress Tracker.
Cheer your kid on when they succeed and point out which mental muscles might need more flexing.

Unique kids. Unique recommendations.

We make it super easy to see which skills need a bit of help and find resources that support their unique needs. You can browse our curated digital and printable resources and even assign them—all with just a few clicks!
